Microstructural Analysis of Mg/CNT Surface Composite Using Friction Stir Processing
Tolety, E., Gupta, A., Singh, N., Saluja, R.K., Gahlot, N.K.
In: Proceedings of the International Conference on Advanced Materials and Manufacturing. Springer, Singapore.
View Publication → doi.org/10.1007/978-981-97-1306-6-15

Abstract Summary

A CNT-reinforced magnesium surface composite was developed using a modified friction stir processing (FSP) technique to achieve improved CNT dispersion within the magnesium matrix. XRF analysis confirmed the elemental presence of carbon, magnesium, and oxygen throughout the composite. SEM imaging demonstrated a consistent and uniform distribution of CNTs across the processed region. Mechanical properties including macro-hardness were measured and analyzed, showing improvement over unreinforced magnesium.
